- Role: UX/UI Designer
   - Key Responsibilities:
     1. Design wireframes, prototypes, and UI elements based on user needs.
     2. Conduct user research and usability testing to refine designs.
     3. Collaborate with developers to ensure accurate implementation of designs.
     4. Stay updated with design trends and best practices.

   - Required Skills:
     1. Proficiency in design tools such as Figma, Adobe XD, or Sketch.
     2. Strong understanding of user experience principles and front-end technologies.

   - Qualifications:
     1. Bachelor's degree in Design, Human-Computer Interaction, or a related field.
