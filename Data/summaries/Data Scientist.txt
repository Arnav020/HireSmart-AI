- Job Title: Data Scientist

   Responsibilities:
     1. Collect, clean, and analyze large datasets.
     2. Develop and deploy machine learning models.
     3. Build predictive analytics solutions to improve business outcomes.
     4. Communicate findings through reports and visualizations using tools like Tableau or Power BI.
     5. Stay updated with advancements in data science and AI.

   Qualifications:
     1. Bachelor's or Master's degree in Data Science, Computer Science, or a related field.
     2. Proficiency in Python, R, SQL, and machine learning frameworks.
     3. Experience with data visualization tools like Tableau or Power BI.
     4. Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
     5. Ability to work independently and in a team environment.
