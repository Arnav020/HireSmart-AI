- Role: Product Manager
   - Responsibilities:
     - Define product vision and strategy based on market research and customer needs.
     - Collaborate with cross-functional teams (engineering, design, marketing) to develop and launch products.
     - Prioritize features, create roadmaps, and manage the product lifecycle.
     - Analyze user feedback and data to optimize product performance.
     - Ensure alignment between business goals and product development.

   - Required Skills:
     - Strong analytical skills for market research and data-driven decision making.
     - Leadership abilities to manage cross-functional teams effectively.
     - Excellent communication skills to articulate product vision and requirements.

   - Qualifications:
     - Bachelor's degree in Business, Computer Science, or a related field.
     - Experience in product management, agile methodologies, and market research.
     - Familiarity with project management tools.
