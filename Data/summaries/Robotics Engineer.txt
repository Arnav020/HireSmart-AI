- Job Title: Robotics Engineer

   Responsibilities:
     1. Design, build, and test robotic systems for industrial or research purposes.
     2. Develop software algorithms for navigation, control, and automation of the robotics.
     3. Integrate sensors, actuators, and AI-based decision-making systems into the robots.
     4. Troubleshoot issues and optimize the performance of the robotic systems.
     5. Stay informed about emerging robotics technologies and advancements.

   Qualifications:
     1. Degree in Robotics, Mechanical Engineering, or a related field with either a Bachelor's or Master's degree.
     2. Proficiency in C++, Python, and ROS (Robot Operating System).
     3. Experience with embedded systems, motion planning, and AI applications in robotics.
     4. Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
