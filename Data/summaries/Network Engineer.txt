- Role: Network Engineer
   - Responsibilities:
     1. Design and deploy network solutions for optimal performance.
     2. Monitor network health and troubleshoot issues as they arise.
     3. Implement security measures to protect network integrity.
     4. Manage firewalls, routers, switches, and VPNs.
     5. Ensure compliance with networking best practices and standards.
   - Required Skills:
     1. Strong knowledge of networking protocols such as TCP/IP, DNS, and DHCP.
     2. Proficiency in Cisco, Juniper, or similar networking technologies.
     3. Problem-solving skills and ability to work under pressure.
   - Qualifications:
     1. Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, IT, or a related field.
     2. Experience with firewalls, routers, and enterprise networking.
