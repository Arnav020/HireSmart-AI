- Role: Data Analyst/Business Intelligence Analyst
   - Key Responsibilities:
     1. Collect, process, and analyze business data to drive insights.
     2. Develop interactive dashboards and reports using BI tools such as Power BI or Tableau.
     3. Collaborate with teams to improve data-driven decision-making processes.
     4. Ensure data accuracy and integrity in reporting.
   - Required Qualifications:
    1. Bachelor's degree in Data Science, Business Analytics, or a related field.
    2. Proficiency in SQL and at least one of the following BI tools: Power BI, Tableau, or similar tools.
    3. Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
    4. Experience in data visualization and statistical analysis.
