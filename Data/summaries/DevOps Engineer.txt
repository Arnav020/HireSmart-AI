- Role: DevOps Engineer
   - Responsibilities:
     - Design, develop, and maintain CI/CD pipelines for seamless deployment.
     - Automate infrastructure management using tools such as Terraform or Ansible.
     - Monitor system performance to ensure high availability and reliability.
     - Collaborate with development and operations teams to optimize workflows.
     - Implement security best practices, ensuring compliance in all systems.
   - Qualifications:
     - Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, IT, or related field.
     - Proficiency in cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, or Google Cloud).
     - Experience with containerization using Docker and Kubernetes.
     - Strong scripting skills including Python, Bash, or PowerShell.
     - Knowledge of configuration management tools like Ansible, Chef, or Puppet.
