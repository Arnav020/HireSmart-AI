- Role: Cloud Engineer
   - Responsibilities:
     1. Design and maintain cloud architecture for high availability and security.
     2. Automate deployments and manage Continuous Integration/Continuous Deployment (CI/CD) pipelines.
     3. Monitor cloud systems, troubleshoot issues, and optimize costs.
     4. Implement security best practices and compliance measures.
   - Required Skills:
     1. Expertise with cloud platforms such as AWS, Azure, or Google Cloud.
     2. Proficiency in infrastructure-as-code (Terraform, CloudFormation).
     3. Strong scripting skills in Python, Bash, or PowerShell.
   - Qualifications:
     1. Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, IT, or a related field.
